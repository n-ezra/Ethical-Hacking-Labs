# WHOIS Lookup Practical

## Overview

This practical demonstrates how to perform a WHOIS lookup using Kali Linux to gather publicly available domain registration information during the reconnaissance phase of a cybersecurity assessment.

WHOIS enumeration helps security analysts, penetration testers, and SOC analysts identify important information about a target domain such as registrar details, DNS infrastructure, registration dates, and ownership-related metadata.

---

# Objective

The objective of this practical is to:

* Perform WHOIS enumeration on a target domain
* Gather publicly available registration details
* Understand the role of WHOIS in reconnaissance
* Analyze the information from a cybersecurity perspective

# Security Relevance

WHOIS information plays an important role during the reconnaissance phase of a cyber attack or penetration test.

Attackers and security professionals may use WHOIS data to:

* Identify hosting providers
* Discover DNS infrastructure
* Enumerate target assets
* Identify registrar information
* Analyze domain age and legitimacy
* Map external infrastructure

Security analysts also use WHOIS during incident investigations and threat intelligence operations.
